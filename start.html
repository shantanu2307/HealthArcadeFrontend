HEALTH ARCADE

How to Play?

  • Allow Access to the Camera.
  • Position yourself infront of the camera such that your whole body is visible on the hologram on the right hand side of the screen.
  • Now you will see that enemies will start appearing on the screen.
  • To kill the enemies, press the spacebar or do an arm curl.
  • On successful execution, the player will change it's direction and will immediately kill the enemy.
  • Keep on playing till you have done a good workout.
  • Share this game with your friends :)
